Pho

Description

pho recipe... straight from Vietnam. Serve with hoisin and sriracha sauce.

Ingridients

  • 10 quarts water
  • 3 pounds chicken bones
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/4 cup rock sugar
  • 1+1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bunch cilantro

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a stockpot. Meanwhile, rinse chicken bones under hot water to get rid of impurities.
  2. Place bones in the pot of boiling water for about 60 minutes
  3. Place whole chicken into the pot and simmer for upto 30-40 minutes.
  4. Combine onion and ginger in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until nicely browned and fragrant.
  5. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add rice noodles and boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 2 to 3 minutes. Drain.
  6. Peel skin off of the cooled chicken; discard skin and bones, reserving the meat.
  7. Serve noodles in bowls topped with chicken meat and broth.
